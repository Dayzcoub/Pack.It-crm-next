# 25 Warehouse — Handout Act Detail Level Note

## 10.157. Handout act detail level

Decision: the handout act should be lightweight by default. Extra item detail is optional and can be enabled for larger teams.

Rules:
- small teams should not fill extra item details during every handout;
- larger companies may enable a stricter act detail mode in settings;
- the default act records the handout fact, users, date, time, items and quantities;
- extended detail mode may add item notes and completeness confirmation.

Purpose:
- keep the default workflow fast;
- avoid extra work for small warehouses;
- support stricter internal control when a company is ready for it.

MVP approach:
- default: simple handout act;
- optional setting: extended item detail in the handout act;
- extended detail mode is not required for MVP operation.
