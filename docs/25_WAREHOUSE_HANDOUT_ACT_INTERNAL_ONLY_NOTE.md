# 25 Warehouse — Handout Act Internal Only Note

## 10.155. Handout act is internal only

Decision: the warehouse handout act is an internal document.

Rules:
- the handout act is not intended for the client;
- it is used by warehouse users, project managers, technicians, and other internal users with proper access;
- it documents the fact of equipment handout inside the company workflow;
- it should not include client-facing wording;
- it should not include prices by default.

Purpose:
- keep warehouse handout control inside the team;
- avoid mixing client documents with internal warehouse documents;
- reduce risk of exposing internal operational information.

MVP approach:
- handout act can be enabled in warehouse settings;
- when enabled, it is generated as an internal PDF/print document;
- no client version is needed at MVP stage.
